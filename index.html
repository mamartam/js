Geralt Of Rivia

Geralt of Rivia is a legendary Witcher from the School of the Wolf. Known as the "White Wolf," he roams the Continent hunting monsters for coin while navigating a world where humans are often more monstrous than the beasts he hunts.
  • Aard: A telekinetic thrust that knocks down enemies.
  • Igni: A blast of fire used to burn foes or light torches.
  • Quen: A protective magical shield that absorbs damage.

  • Steel Sword: For humans and non-magical beasts.
  • Silver Sword: Specifically crafted for monsters.
  • Witcher Armor: Reinforced leather providing agility and protection.
